Todays' Recipe

Milky Pasta Delight

Cut veggies and make tomato paste of 2 tomatos. Boil pasta. Add salt and oil in it. Stir fry veggies like peppers carrots onions. In separate pan, add 3 tablespoon butter and then add 3 tablespoons of flour. Cook until light brown. Add tomato paste in flour wala pan and cook for 3-4 minutes. Add spices (salt, red chilli, teaspoon of vinegar, shahi paneer masala) Cook for 2-3 minutes. Add milk or cream. Cook on medium flame until getting thick. Stir in between. Drain all water from pasta. Add pasta.
